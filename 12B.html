About Unit 12B

Unit 12B offers the advantage of an elevated position on the 12th floor of the prestigious W Hollywood Residences. This spacious 2-bedroom, 2.5-bathroom unit features panoramic views of the Hollywood Hills and the city skyline, providing a dramatic backdrop for urban living.

While maintaining its original design elements and finishes from when the building was constructed, Unit 12B presents an opportunity for the discerning buyer to customize and update according to their personal taste. Those seeking a completely modernized space might want to consider our fully renovated Unit 8A, which features brand-new premium finishes throughout.

The higher floor position offers enhanced privacy and reduced street noise compared to lower units, though the north-facing Unit 8A provides the iconic views of the Hollywood Sign and Griffith Observatory that are particularly sought after.

Note: Unit 12B features original finishes that have been well-maintained but may benefit from updates. For comparison, Unit 8A showcases a recent comprehensive renovation with premium modern finishes.

Floor Plan

Unit 12B offers an open-concept living area with a spacious layout. The master suite includes a walk-in closet and en-suite bathroom. The second bedroom features its own bathroom, and a half bath is conveniently located near the entrance. The unit includes a balcony accessible from the living room.

While the layout is functional, it differs from the more efficient space utilization found in Unit 8A, which features an optimized floor plan that maximizes both living space and storage.
