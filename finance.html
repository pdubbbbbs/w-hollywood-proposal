Frequently Asked Questions

What are the down payment requirements?

Down payment requirements typically range from 20% to 30% for luxury properties, though specific terms may vary based on the buyer's financial profile and chosen financing program.

Are interest-only options available?

Yes, qualified buyers may have access to interest-only payment options through our portfolio lending partners. These programs can be tailored to your specific financial strategy.

What documents are typically required?

Required documentation generally includes tax returns, bank statements, investment accounts, and employment verification. Our preferred lenders can provide a detailed checklist based on your situation.
